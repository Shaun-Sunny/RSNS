HEALING HANDS – CARING HEARTS

May we be guided by the light of compassion, the warmth of empathy, and the wisdom of experience. Let us approach each patient with reverence, recognizing the sacredness of their journey and the privilege of being invited into their lives during moments of vulnerability.

A Nurse's Call: Compassionate Care, Support Healing, Inspire Hope

  • ➲ Caregivers: Nurses are the epitome of compassion, providing care that goes beyond medical treatments to comfort and support patients in their times of need.
  • ➲ Empathy in Action: Nurses embody empathy in action, putting themselves in their patients' shoes and providing care with genuine understanding and kindness.

Message from Principal’s Desk

"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others"

- Mahatma Gandhi

RSM School of Nursing is a distinguished institution rooted in the rich tradition of Catholic values. We are dedicated to fostering excellence in nursing education, research, and clinical practice. Our commitment to providing a supportive and innovative learning environment ensures that our students are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in the dynamic field of healthcare. We embrace the values of compassion, service, and excellence, inspired by the teachings of Jesus, the great teacher. As the Principal of this institution, I am honored to nurture both the minds and spirits of our students. Our rigorous academic programs empower our students to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those they care for. We believe that healthcare is not merely a profession but a sacred calling, and we are committed to instilling in our students a deep sense of responsibility towards even the most vulnerable members of our society.

Rino Simonetti Memorial School of Nursing

RSM School of Nursing is recognized by the Indian Nursing Council and the Meghalaya Nursing Council for the revised General Nursing & Midwifery Course of three years.
