Message from the Principal’s Desk

"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others."
— Mahatma Gandhi

RSM School of Nursing is a distinguished institution rooted in the rich tradition of Catholic values. We are dedicated to fostering excellence in nursing education, research, and clinical practice. Our commitment to providing a supportive and innovative learning environment ensures that our students are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values essential to excel in the dynamic field of healthcare.

We embrace the values of compassion, service, and excellence, inspired by the teachings of Jesus, the great teacher. As the Principal of this institution, I am honored to nurture both the minds and spirits of our students. Our rigorous academic programs empower our students to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those they care for. We believe that healthcare is not merely a profession but a sacred calling, and we are committed to instilling in our students a deep sense of responsibility, especially towards the most vulnerable members of our society.

Together, let us continue to uphold the values of compassion and excellence in nursing education and in our clinical field.

R.S.M School of Nursing

R.S.M School of Nursing is recognized by the Indian as well as the Meghalaya Nursing Councils for the revised General Nursing & Midwifery Course of three years.
