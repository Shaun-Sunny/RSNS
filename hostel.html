Hostel

School Information

General Instructions

  1. The student shall dress neatly and appear decent at all times in the school, hospital, and community field.
  2. Strict discipline is to be observed in the school, hostel, and the hospital premises.
  3. Communal harmony is to be preserved and fostered by all in ‘word and deed’.
  4. The students have to attend all the spiritual activities according to the institutional horrarium.
  5. The student who gets married or pregnant during the course of study will not be allowed to continue the course further.
  6. Going out of the institution for any purpose shall be done only with the knowledge and permission of the principal or warden, in accordance with the rules of the hostel.
  7. Possession of mobile phones, radio, and any electronic devices are strictly forbidden and if found, firm action will be taken.
  8. Use of alcohol and drugs is strictly forbidden in the hostel and campus and strict action will be taken by the managing committee.
  9. Misconduct, disobedience, inefficiency, or negligence of study or duty is sufficient ground for dismissal from the Nursing course.
  10. Any student, who discontinues the course for any reason at any time, will be liable to pay a sum of ₹75,000 in compensation for the vacated seat.
  11. All terms and conditions set forth in the Prospectus are liable to be revised whenever found necessary. The Management reserves the right to make necessary changes in the rules and regulations of the Institution.

Hostel Policies

  • Visitation Policies: There are set guidelines for visitation hours and who is allowed to visit the hosteler in the hostel.
  • Quiet Hours: During which noise levels should be kept to a minimum to allow for studying and rest.
  • Room Maintenance: Requires hostelers to keep their rooms clean and tidy, with regular inspections conducted.
  • Respect for Others: We teach and emphasize the importance of respecting fellow hostelers' privacy, belongings, and personal space.
  • Safety and Security: We implement measures to ensure the safety and security of students, such as locking doors at night and providing emergency contact information.
  • Alcohol and Substance Use: Clearly outlines policies regarding the consumption of alcohol and other substances within the hostel premises.
  • Internet and Technology Usage: Establishes guidelines for the use of internet and electronic devices to promote responsible usage and respect for others' access.
  • Personal Conduct: Encourages students to conduct themselves in a respectful and professional manner at all times.
  • Health and Hygiene: Promotes good hygiene practices and provides information on healthcare resources available to the students.
  • Counselling and Support: Provides support and guidance to residents who may be experiencing personal or academic challenges, including referrals to counselling services or academic advisors.
  • Emergency Procedures: Educates students on what to do in case of emergencies such as fire, medical emergencies, or natural disasters.
  • Community Engagement: Encourages students to participate in hostel activities, community service, and other opportunities for personal and professional development.
  • Compliance: Clearly communicates that students are expected to comply with all hostel rules and regulations and outlines consequences for violations.

Hostel Accommodation

The course is strictly residential. All students will be provided hostel accommodation in the school campus.

As you step into our hostel, you will find a warm and inclusive environment where students from diverse backgrounds come together in pursuit of their nursing education. Our rooms are designed to provide comfort and privacy, ensuring that you have a peaceful haven to rest and recharge after a day of rigorous study and clinical practice. Here, lifelong friendships are forged, where students support and uplift each other through the challenges of academic life. Our common areas are vibrant hubs of activity, where you can unwind, socialize, and engage in meaningful conversations with your peers. Being the part of a Catholic institution, we offer opportunities for prayer, Holy Mass and spiritual guidance and counselling, providing a supportive environment for students to nurture their faith and deepen their connection to something greater than themselves.

Transport

We have a school bus for providing safe, reliable, and efficient transportation during our academic-related journeys. This enhances easy access to essential clinical experiences and community engagements, together with promoting inclusivity and safety. The bus service of the school addresses multiple needs, contributing to a more effective educational environment.
