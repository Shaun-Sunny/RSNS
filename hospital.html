Hospital

Holy Cross Hospital, Walbakgre, Tura

The hospital started functioning in the year 1993 under the auspices of Holy Cross Congregation. Holy Cross Hospital is a Multi bedded, fabulously equipped hospital established in the year 1993 in Tura, West Garo Hills, Meghalaya. It is the fulfillment of a great dream of Most Rev. George Mamlassery, the then Bishop of Tura diocese, Meghalaya. The inadequate health care facilities in Garo Hills had invoked in his grace a missionary zeal to construct a 150 bedded hospital. The hospital has been a beacon of transformative impact, weaving hope and healing into the lives of hundreds of thousands of people from far and near. It delivers accessible and profound services in focused and caring environment with advanced facilities for minimally invasive and short stay surgeries, Obstetrics & Gynaecology and Pediatric & Neonatal care. The hospital invariably offers affordable access to various medical and surgical specialties. The diligent services fastened onto Christian values without compromising professional standards, blending latest technology and ingenious procedures remains focused on outcomes to usher in healthy and happy days.

Rev. George Mamlassery, Bishop Emeritus, Diocese of Tura, Meghalaya
(1932-2024)
