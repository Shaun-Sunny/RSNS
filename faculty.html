Holy Cross Hospital, Walbakgre, Tura

The hospital started functioning in the year 1993 under the auspices of Holy Cross Congregation. It is the fulfilment of a great dream of Most Rev. George Mamlassery, the Bishop of Tura Meghalaya. The inadequate health care facilities existing in Garo Hills had invoked in his grace a missionary zeal to construct a 100-bed hospital. The hospital was inaugurated by the Parliamentarian Shri Purno A Sangma. Holy Cross Hospital is the most sought after medical institution in West Garo Hills.

Rt. Rev. George Mamlassery, Bishop Emeritus, Tura

Holy Cross Hospital is a multi bedded, fabulously equipped hospital established in the year 1993 in Tura, West Garo Hills, Meghalaya, under the auspices of Holy Cross Congregation. It is the fulfilment of a great dream of Most Rev. George Mamlassery, the Bishop of Tura Meghalaya. The inadequate health care facilities existing in Garo Hills had invoked in his grace a missionary zeal to construct a 100-bed hospital. The hospital stands as a beacon of transformative impact, weaving hope and healing into the lives of hundreds of thousands of people from far and near. With advanced facilities for minimally invasive and short-stay surgeries, the hospital, delivers accessible and profound services in a focused and caring environment. It offers affordable access to various medical and surgical specialties. Our diligent service focused on Christian values without compromising professional standards blending latest technology and ingenious procedures remains focused on outcomes to usher in healthy and happy days.
